# Pipeline: Quarterly Business Report

## Overview
This pipeline produces a polished quarterly business report from raw financial and operational data provided by the user. It structures the input data, analyses performance against the prior year, drafts a narrative executive summary, and assembles everything into a single formatted markdown report ready for review or distribution.

## Audience
Senior leadership and board members. The tone should be professional, precise, and data-driven. Assume familiarity with business metrics but not with the underlying data systems.
